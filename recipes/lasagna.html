Lasagna Recipe

Description

Start by making the meat sauce. In a pan, heat some oil and sauté chopped onion and garlic until soft. Add minced meat (beef or a beef–pork mix) and cook until browned. Stir in tomato sauce (or crushed tomatoes), a bit of tomato paste, salt, pepper, and Italian herbs like oregano and basil. Let it simmer for about 15–20 minutes so the flavors deepen. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese (or a thick white sauce), a little salt, pepper, and optionally an egg and some grated cheese. Boil lasagna sheets if they are not oven-ready.

To assemble, spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of lasagna sheets, then ricotta mixture, then meat sauce, and sprinkle mozzarella or grated cheese. Repeat the layers (usually 3 times). Finish with meat sauce and a generous cheese layer on top. Cover with foil and bake at about 180°C for 25–30 minutes, then uncover and bake 10 more minutes until golden and bubbly. Let it rest 10–15 minutes before cutting so it holds its shape.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Minced beef (or beef–pork mix)
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Sauté chopped onion and garlic in oil, then add minced meat and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and herbs; simmer 15–20 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta (or béchamel) with seasoning and egg if using.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  6. Add lasagna sheets, then ricotta mixture, then meat sauce, and cheese.
  7. Repeat layers 2–3 times, finishing with sauce and cheese on top.
  8. Cover with foil and bake 25–30 minutes, then uncover and bake 10 more minutes.
  9. Let it rest 10–15 minutes before cutting and serving.
