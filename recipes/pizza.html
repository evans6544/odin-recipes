Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Description

Start by preparing the pizza dough if making from scratch, or use a ready-made pizza base. Spread a layer of tomato pizza sauce evenly over the surface. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese on top. Add slices of pepperoni evenly across the pizza. You can also add a pinch of dried oregano or chili flakes for extra flavor.

Preheat the oven to a high temperature (around 220°C). Place the pizza on a baking tray or pizza stone and bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and serve hot.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough or pizza base
  • Tomato pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil
  • Dried oregano (optional)
  • Chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough or place the ready-made base on a tray.
  3. Spread tomato pizza sauce evenly over the base.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Add pepperoni slices evenly.
  6. Drizzle a little olive oil and add oregano or chili flakes if desired.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and serve.
